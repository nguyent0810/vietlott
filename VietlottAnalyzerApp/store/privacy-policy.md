# Privacy Policy for Vietlott Analyzer

**Last updated: December 30, 2024**

## Introduction

Vietlott Analyzer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Prediction Data**: Numbers you save and prediction preferences
- **App Settings**: Your lottery type preferences, notification settings, and app configurations
- **User Feedback**: Any feedback or support requests you send us

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Performance Data**: Crash reports and app performance metrics (anonymized)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Personal Identity**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- **Financial Information**: We do not collect payment or banking information
- **Location Data**: We do not track your location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your contacts or personal files

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide App Functionality**: Store your predictions and preferences locally
- **Improve User Experience**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features
- **App Maintenance**: Fix bugs and improve app performance
- **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Most of your data is stored locally on your device
- You have full control over your local data
- Data can be exported or deleted at any time through app settings

### Cloud Services
- We use secure cloud services only for:
  - Fetching public lottery results
  - App analytics (anonymized)
  - Crash reporting (anonymized)

### Security Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
- We implement industry-standard security practices
- Regular security audits and updates

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or legal process
- **Safety**: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
- **Service Providers**: Anonymized data with trusted analytics providers

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses these third-party services:
- **Expo/React Native**: App development platform
- **GitHub**: For fetching public lottery data
- **Google Analytics**: Anonymized usage analytics
- **Crashlytics**: Anonymized crash reporting

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app through settings
- **Export**: Export your data for backup purposes
- **Delete**: Clear all app data at any time
- **Control**: Manage notification and privacy settings
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics and crash reporting

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymized data retained for up to 2 years
- **Support Data**: Deleted after resolving your inquiry

## International Users

Our app is designed for Vietnamese lottery systems but can be used internationally. Data processing may occur in various countries where our service providers operate.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through:
- App notifications
- Updated policy in app settings
- App store update notes

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- **Google Play Store Policies**
- **Vietnamese Data Protection Laws**
- **International Privacy Standards**

By using Vietlott Analyzer, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our data practices as described herein.
